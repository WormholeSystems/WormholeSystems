# Killmails

The map surfaces recent **killmails** in and around your chain so you can read the room — where fights are happening, where a gatecamp might be sitting, and which systems are hot right now. Kill data comes from **zKillboard** and arrives in real time, so new kills appear as they happen.

## Filtering the feed

The **Killmails** panel has three filters:

- **All killmails** — everything in and around the chain.
- **J-Space killmails** — wormhole systems only.
- **K-Space killmails** — known-space systems only.

Each kill links out to zKillboard for the full details.

Use the feed to spot a forming gang, confirm a hole is quiet before committing, or keep tabs on a neighbour's activity. For a longer-term read on how dangerous a system is, see [Threat analysis](/documentation/intel/threat-analysis).
