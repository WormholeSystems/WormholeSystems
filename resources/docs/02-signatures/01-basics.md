# Signature basics

Each system tracks its **signatures** — the cosmic signatures you see in the in-game probe scanner, identified by a code like `ABC-123` (three letters, a dash, three digits).

A signature on the map records:

- its **signature ID** (e.g. `ABC-123`)
- its **category**
- its **type** (the specific site, once resolved) — or a free-text name for temporary event sites
- for wormholes, an optional link to the **connection** it represents, plus mass, ship-size, and lifetime status

## Why it matters

Signatures are how the chain stays trustworthy. A fully-scanned system with every signature resolved tells your group there are no surprise wormholes left to find — and a system with unresolved signatures flags work still to do.

The fastest way to enter them is [pasting your scan results](/documentation/signatures/pasting-scan-results).
