# Aliases & occupier

Wormhole systems have unmemorable catalog names like `J123456`. Annotations make the chain readable.

## Alias

Give any system a custom **alias** — "Home", "Staging", "C5-static", "Gank hole" — and it reads at a glance instead of by its catalog ID. Aliases are shared on the map, so everyone uses the same names for the same systems.

## Occupier

Record an **occupier** note for who lives in a system — the corp or alliance holding it, or a label like "Blue Badgers". It's a quick way to capture intel about the chain's neighbours without leaving the map.

Combine aliases and occupier notes with [system status](/documentation/organizing-your-map/system-status) to turn a wall of `J`-codes into a chain you can actually read.
