# Character presence

When tracking is on, the map shows where your group's characters are **in real time** — and that same live location is what powers [mapping as you fly](/documentation/getting-started/auto-mapping).

## What you see

For each tracked character:

- the **system** they're currently in
- their **ship type and name**
- their **online status** and when they were last online

A background process polls EVE for the locations of online characters and broadcasts changes to everyone viewing the map, so positions update on their own within a short interval — no refreshing.

## It's opt-in, per map

Two things must be true for a character to appear:

1. **Scopes** — the character has granted the location, ship, and online scopes (see [Connecting your character](/documentation/getting-started/connecting-your-character)).
2. **Consent** — you've turned on location sharing **for that specific map** (the "allow the map to use your location" toggle).

Consent is per-map, so you can share your location on your corp's home chain while staying invisible on another map. Turn it off and you immediately disappear from that map. Seeing other people's positions also requires [Member](/documentation/access-and-permissions/roles) access or higher.
